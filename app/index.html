The Old Church Restaurant introduces her glamorous sister...

The Villa is the perfect place for a gathering of family, friend or business colleagues.

The beautifully restored historic Villa is steeped in history and full of character..... A perfect place to spend a winters evening.

Have a pre-dinner drink in front of the fire and then move into one of the three intimate dining spaces or down to the cellar to enjoy a wonderful menu and wine list designed to compliment the glamour of the Villa.

Private rooms are available for groups of 6 - 12. Of course, big sister "The Old Church" is right next door and will continue to host larger groups, functions and weddings.

Come and visit the new experience in town.... We promise you won't be disappointed!
