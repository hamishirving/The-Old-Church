The Restaurant

Soaring ceilings and a magnificent chandelier creating breathtaking impact for your reception or indoor ceremony.

The Old Church features soaring ceilings and a magnificent chandelier creating breathtaking impact for your reception or indoor ceremony. The Old Church is flanked by The Conservatory, which in turn opens out onto The Courtyard Garden and Bar.

The three complementary spaces can be used together or independently to cater for guest lists and functions of all sizes and types.

Seats up to 150 people.

The Conservatory

A light and airy space with stunning views.

A light and airy space with stunning views through the delicate arched windows across the Courtyard Garden and Bar to the vineyards and ranges beyond.

Seats up to 40 people.

Courtyard Garden

Enjoy a sunny al-fresco lunch or a magical dinner sheltered by the fairy-lit hedges.

Enjoy a sunny al-fresco lunch or a magical dinner sheltered by the fairy-lit hedges enclosing our elegant courtyard.

Complete with its own bar, the Courtyard also transforms into a fantastic summer dance-floor!

Seats up to 60 people
