The Old Church Villa

Glamorous sister to The Old Church Restaurant.

The historic Old Church Villa also offers several different spaces, including an outside area.

In winter it transforms into a cosy lunch and dinner spot. Savour a drink in front of the roaring fire and then move to one of four intimate dining rooms to enjoy your meal.

Private rooms seat 6-12 people for lunch or dinner.

The Summer Marquee

Coming soon!

Summer is private function season at The Old Church and The Old Church Villa but with the addition of the Summer Marquee you can still enjoy our fine food, service and atmosphere.

Create your own delicious platter for lunch, enjoy our Chef’s Blackboard Special with a wine or beer for just $38 on Wednesday and Thursday nights, or dine ‘a la carte’ on Friday and Saturday nights.
