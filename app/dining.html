The Villa

As the latest dining experience in Hawkes Bay, The Old Church Villa is the perfect place for a gathering of friends, family or business colleagues.

The beautifully restored historic Villa is steeping in history and full of character... A perfect place to spend a winters evening.

Have a pre-dinner drink in front of the fire and then move into one of the 4 intimate dining spaces or down to the cellar to enjoy a wonderful menu and wine list designed to compliment the glamour of the Villa.

Private villa rooms are available for groups of 6-12. The Cellar holds up to 15 and of course big sister "The Old Church" is right next door and will continue to host larger groups, functions and weddings.

Come and visit the new experience in town... we promise you won't be disappointed!

The Old Church

The vision for The Old Church has been realised by creating an unsurpassed blend of history and style. Whether it is a wedding, a large function or a more relaxed dining experience. The Old Church can accommodate all. Enjoy lunch in the conservatory or dine out in the garden. Enjoy a platter and a selection of wines from our 100% Hawkes Bay wine list.

Step it up for a special occasion and dine under the magnificent chandelier.

Set amongst the vines, with a stunning aspect to the Ruahines, Kawekes and the setting sun you can be certain that whatever way you choose to experience The Old Church it will result in fabulous food, wines, service and atmosphere. The unique experience of The Old Church will be a memory to enjoy for years to come.

The Church can cater for up to 140 people for a formal sit down meal. For groups of up to 45 the conservatory makes a special venue. Then there is the option of outdoor dining in our courtyard garden with its own outdoor bar.
