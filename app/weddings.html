Venue

Soaring ceilings and a magnificent chandelier creating breathtaking impact for your reception or indoor ceremony.

The Old Church features soaring ceilings and a magnificent chandelier creating breathtaking impact for your reception or indoor ceremony. The Old Church is flanked by The Conservatory, which in turn opens out onto The Courtyard Garden and Bar.

The three complementary spaces can be used exclusively for weddings of 60-150 guests.

Intimate weddings of up to 40 guests can have private use of the Conservatory and Courtyard areas.

Set amongst the grapevines, with stunning views of the Ruahine and Kaweka ranges, The Old Church is a place where fabulous memories are made.

Packages

Take away the stress and guesswork with our special packages for your special day.

Our on-site Wedding Planner will support you from the moment you book.

Your fabulous wedding package includes:

  • Exclusive use of the venue from 3.30 pm to 1.00 am for your wedding plus access during the week prior for your wedding rehearsal.
  • Floor plan options and templates to help you arrange seating.
  • Reception set up including tableware, black or white linen tablecloths and napkins, access to centerpieces from The Old Church’s inventory (or you can supply your own).
  • Ceremony set up including stunning indoor and outdoor options.
  • Printing of menus, seating chart and table numbers (or you can supply your own).
  • Use of our picturesque buildings, gardens and vineyard for photographs and a wedding hamper of canapés and drinks for you to enjoy while having photographs taken.
  • Use of our sound system for your ceremony and dinner music as well as microphone for speeches.
  • A full planning guide including recommendations for entertainment, florists, photographers, wedding celebrants, accommodation and more. Plus we will provide courtesy meals for up to three of your external wedding professionals.
  • You will also select from our comprehensive wedding menu.

Menu

Regularly updated to capture the best flavours of each season.

Your meal plan includes:

  • Complimentary round of bubbles (Tier One Package).
  • Selection of 4 canapes.
  • Bread and olive oil.
  • Choice of 3 Entrees (Tier One Package).
  • Choice of 3 Mains
  • Salad and vegetable side dishes
  • Choice of 3 Desserts
  • Tea and coffee buffet

Our selection of fine wines and popular beers are charged on a consumption basis. Sorry, we cannot offer BYO.

To cater for your smaller guests, we offer a children’s menu for $50 each, and under-5s are free.

Our no-stress wedding packages cost as little as $105 per person.
