Dinner

An experience like no other; our courses menu is a unique concept for A la carte dining.

Only ten minutes from Napier, with several lovingly restored spaces catering for groups from 6 to 140, The Old Church offers warm hospitality, luxurious décor, exquisite food and wines selected from the best of Hawke’s Bay’s boutique vineyards.

Whether your event is large or small, our experienced team will take care of every detail so you can simply relax and enjoy yourself.

Set amongst the grape vines, with stunning views of the Ruahine and Kaweka Ranges, The Old Church is a place where fabulous memories are made.
